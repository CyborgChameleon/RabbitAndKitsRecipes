Pizza

Living in northern america, everyone knows the pain for dry and thin pizza with pitiful crust of the fast food chains in northern america. My relative's growing experience in Argentina puts such pizza to shame.

Instead of suffering from the ny's pizza, my family attempted to follow the following recipe

https://sugarspunrun.com/the-best-pizza-dough-recipe

But after experimenting a bit she changed it a bit so the alternative recipe is the following.

Ingredients:

Dough
  • 4-4.5 cup of flour
  • 4.5 tsp yeast
  • 3 tsp sugar
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 2tsp dried basilic
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 350ml warm water
Sauce
  • 1tbsp tomato paste
  • 2tbsp olive tapenade
  • 4tbsp tostitos salsa
Toppings(add and mix to taste)
  • olive
  • rosted pickled red pepper
  • ham
  • cheese

Instruction:

  1. Throughly mix 2 cup of flour and the rest of the dough ingredient
  2. Slowly add flour until you can make a ball of dough with it
  3. Let it rise 30 min in a moist warm spot and covered(we left it in the oven with a bowl of hot water)
  4. Oil up your pan and your hands. Put the risen dough on the oiled pan and gently squeeze out the air and spread it out (no kneading)
  5. Let it rise for another 20 min
  6. Preheat to 215C
  7. Poke holes with the fork and pop any bubbles and mix your sauce and then spread it
  8. Bake for 10min
  9. Add your toppings
  10. Bake another 10 mind or until your cheese are nice and gooey
